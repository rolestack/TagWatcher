Recommended Handover Workflow

  1. Configure OIDC above and enable SSO.
  2. Log out and sign in via SSO with your organization account.
  3. Come back as local admin, go to Users & Groups, and grant Admin to your SSO user.
  4. Confirm SSO admin access works, then deactivate or delete the local admin account.
